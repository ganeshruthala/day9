12th fail movie

About movie

            Based on Anurag Pathak`s bestselling novel of the same name, 12th Fail depicts the true story of 
            an IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma hailing from a small town in Chambal, who fearlessly embraced 
            the idea of restarting his academic journey and reclaiming his destiny at a place where millions
             of students attempt for the world`s toughest competitive exam, UPSC. The film is not just a chronicle
              of one man`s journey but a celebration of everyone who dared to look at failures as an opportunity to #Restart.
